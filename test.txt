To derive the launch velocity $v_0$ in terms of the max range $d_{\text{max}}$, we will use equation \eqref{eq:max-range-optimum-angle} and equation \eqref{eq:launch-relative-velocity}.

Starting with equation \eqref{eq:max-range-optimum-angle}:

\begin{equation}
d_{\text{max}} = \frac{v_0^2}{g} \left/ \left(1 - \frac{1}{2}\tilde{v}^2 \right) \right.
\end{equation}

We can substitute the value of $\tilde{v}$ from equation \eqref{eq:launch-relative-velocity}:

\begin{equation}
\tilde{v} = \frac{v_0}{\sqrt{Rg}}
\end{equation}

Square both sides:

\begin{equation}
\tilde{v}^2 = \frac{v_0^2}{Rg}
\end{equation}

Substituting this into our main equation:

\begin{equation}
d_{\text{max}} = \frac{v_0^2}{g} \left/ \left(1 - \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{v_0^2}{Rg} \right) \right.
\end{equation}

Multiply everything by $g$:

\begin{equation}
d_{\text{max}} \cdot g = v_0^2 \left/ \left(1 - \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{v_0^2}{Rg} \right) \right.
\end{equation}

Multiply everything by the denominator:

\begin{equation}
d_{\text{max}} \cdot g \left(1 - \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{v_0^2}{Rg} \right) = v_0^2
\end{equation}

Expanding:

\begin{equation}
d_{\text{max}} \cdot g - \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{d_{\text{max}} \cdot g \cdot v_0^2}{Rg} = v_0^2
\end{equation}

Multiplying everything by $Rg$:

\begin{equation}
d_{\text{max}} \cdot R \cdot g^2 - \frac{1}{2} \cdot d_{\text{max}} \cdot g \cdot v_0^2 = v_0^2 \cdot Rg
\end{equation}

Now, collect terms and solve for $v_0^2$:

\begin{equation}
v_0^2 \cdot Rg + \frac{1}{2} \cdot d_{\text{max}} \cdot g \cdot v_0^2 = d_{\text{max}} \cdot R \cdot g^2
\end{equation}

Factor out $v_0^2$:

\begin{equation}
v_0^2 \left( Rg + \frac{1}{2} \cdot d_{\text{max}} \cdot g \right) = d_{\text{max}} \cdot R \cdot g^2
\end{equation}

Finally, solve for $v_0^2$:

\begin{equation}
v_0^2 = \frac{d_{\text{max}} \cdot R \cdot g^2}{Rg + \frac{1}{2} \cdot d_{\text{max}} \cdot g}
\end{equation}

Thus, the launch velocity $v_0$ in terms of max range $d_{\text{max}}$ is:

\begin{equation}
v_0 = \sqrt{\frac{d_{\text{max}} \cdot R \cdot g^2}{Rg + \frac{1}{2} \cdot d_{\text{max}} \cdot g}}
\end{equation}

This is the expression for the launch velocity in terms of the maximum range.
